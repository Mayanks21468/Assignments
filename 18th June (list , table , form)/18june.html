Wings of Fire

About the Book

Wings of Fire is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India. The book highlights his journey from a small-town boy in Rameswaram to becoming one of India's top scientists. It inspires readers with themes of determination, vision, and patriotism. The story is motivating for students and youth across the country.
